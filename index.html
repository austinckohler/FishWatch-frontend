Find a Fish!

Find a Fish, is a web application designed to allow users to find a species of salt water fish. Users can browse the home page in to find a picture of a fish they might recognize and click on its name to get more information. Users may also sort the through fish by their ................... We understand fishers may want to get more information about a species such as the location in which they reside or maybe learn more about their habitat. Our goal is to provide users with a more robust fish finding experience. We hope you enjoy!

Find a Fish!

Find a Fish, is a web application designed to allow users to find species of salt water fish. Users can browse the home page to find a picture of a fish they might recognize and click its name for information about the species, habitat, or location.

Users also have the ability to search fish by name. With so many species of fish, and many that are not listed, we decided to provide our users with the "Enter a New Fish Species" feature. In order to sucessfully add a fish you will need to provide: species name, scientific name, habitat, location, and an image URL.

Our goal is to provide users with a more robust fish finding experience. Enjoy!
